TAKE A LOOK INTO HOW IT'S DONE

  • Bring It On

You won't need to worry for long. We will get the car you love back to looking just as good as new. Whether you hit something or someone hit you we can handle your case start to finish so you don't have to stress about the details.
Once you bring in your car, we can estimate the damage and give you the information you need to get your claim rolling, or to give you an amount to pay it yourself. After you have made the decision to get your car fixed with us, then we can figure out a rental situation, payment, appointment date, and even a ride home.

After we get the paperwork set and ready to go, then we can get into the fun part of the job; the repair. We went ahead and ordered the parts and as they trickle in, we can remove the damage to your vehicle and get a closer look on what is going on under there. Sometimes, and in most cases there is additional damage to the vehicle that was missed on not accounted for. But don't worry, our customer service team can get a supplement going in no time.
The repair itself could take a few days but we work efficiently to make sure you get the car back in an honest amount of time. After the tear down is complete and the parts come in, we begin the paint process and then on to the rebuild.

This is where your car comes out of cosmetic surgery and can finally get the clean up it deserves. We have the best man on the job to buff out any little bumps and scrapes, to polish the outside and make it shine like new, and to give it a terrific car wash to return it to factory quality. And once the process is complete, we go over the car again to check lights, horn, and signals.
It is a long process, but a good one. One that has been used for over forty years and will continue to be used. We have rave reviews for our service and that's what makes our customers come back to us time and time again.
