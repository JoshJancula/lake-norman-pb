Our Mission

We want to be the car body maintenance and repair facility you turn to for all your automotive needs. For the many years we’ve been in business, we have been striving to provide our clients the best quality service possible to gain their full satisfaction. And they’ve shown their appreciation by coming back to us repeatedly.

We want nothing more than to keep you and your vehicle safe and damage free. Whether you own an import, a domestic, or a high performance exotic car, we’re the place to take your car for dents and windshield cracks, extended warranty work, body repairs, or even a simple touch up.
